App Manual :

 

Intro Slider :

If you open app for the first time you will see a tutorial. You can see it further either by swiping left or right or pressing next button or else you can skip it.

Phone Number Verification :

On this page Enter your mobile number here and press on next.

OTP :

Enter the otp received by message .As for now enter 1111 as otp.

Registration:

Enter your full name and email(Optional field) on this page and press save button  to move further.

  Home Page:

You will see is app homepage where all posted complaints by you will be shown. Allow access to all permissions by clicking on  "allow" button to use app.

Click on “Add complaint” button to post complaint. To detect your current location, location permission must be granted.You can do that by pressing on "ok".

Upload Complaint Page:

  • To take the photo of the grievance (Anomaly) click on camera icon .
  • Then select the appropriate grievance(Complaint category) by clicking on “select grievance ”.
  • After That if you want to add description regarding your complaint you can enter it in add description field.
  • The next step is posting your complaint. You can do that by clicking on “Upload ” button.

Error might occur while uploading :

  1. "This road is not under jurisdiction of R&B department": if you see such message that means either you are too far away from the road or the road you posted from is not under R&B's jurisdiction.
  2. "No officer found on given road": Please notify us on number mentioned at end of manual if you get this message.

Save Complaint Offline:

If you don't have  active internet connection at the time of posting complain, you can  save the complaint offline and upload it after getting an active internet connection.

Complaint Details

(After Successfully Uploaded) :

If your complaint is successfully posted you will be redirected to homepage and can see posted complaint.

By clicking on that complaint you can further see the complaint details.

Notifications :

On homepage By clicking on bell icon you will be redirected to notification page where you will receive notifications of your posted complaint.

Information Of Updated Complaints :

if there are any notifications regarding your complaint you will see a card containing complaint status and other details of the complaint. By clicking on the card  or " i " on top right corner to see the complaint details

Options :

On home page by clicking on menu button which is on bottom bar you will see 3 fields.

  • Contact us : Details about our company and R&B department .  
  • Help : To understand features of the app.
  • Logout : To get logged out from the app .
